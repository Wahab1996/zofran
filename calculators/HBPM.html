  • Assess ABCs: Airway (inhalation injury?), Breathing, Circulation (shock risk).
  • Determine depth: Superficial, partial thickness, full thickness.
  • Estimate TBSA: Use Rule of Nines or Lund-Browder in children.
  • Fluid resuscitation: If >10% TBSA in children or >15% TBSA in adults (Parkland Formula: 4 mL/kg/%TBSA).
  • Pain control & infection prevention: Analgesia, tetanus prophylaxis, wound care.
  • Consider referral: Burns >10% TBSA, face/hands/genitalia, inhalation, electrical, or circumferential burns.
