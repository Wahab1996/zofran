🧪 ABG Quick Interpretation

  • pH >7.45 → Alkalosis | pH <7.35 → Acidosis
  • PaCO₂ >45 → Respiratory acidosis | <35 → Respiratory alkalosis
  • HCO₃⁻ >26 → Metabolic alkalosis | <22 → Metabolic acidosis
  • Example: pH 7.30, PaCO₂ 50, HCO₃⁻ 24 → Respiratory acidosis
