Screening Guidelines – Saudi Arabia

Breast Cancer
Women 40–74: Mammography every 1–2 years. Earlier if family history. Stop if life expectancy <10 yrs.
USPSTF 2024, MOH
Cervical Cancer
Women 21–29: Pap every 3 yrs. Women 30–65: Pap+HPV every 5 yrs. Stop after 65 if adequate.
USPSTF 2023
Colorectal Cancer
Adults 45–75: Annual FIT or colonoscopy every 10 yrs. Continue to 85 selectively.
USPSTF 2021
Diabetes Mellitus (Type 2)
Adults ≥35 or ≥18 if risk factors. Repeat every 3 yrs if normal.
USPSTF 2021, MOH
Hypertension
Adults ≥18: Check BP at least every 1–2 yrs if normal.
USPSTF, ACC/AHA
Dyslipidemia
Men ≥35, women ≥45: Lipid panel every 5 yrs. Earlier if risk factors.
USPSTF
Obesity
All ≥6 yrs: Screen using BMI. Counsel if BMI ≥25.
USPSTF, WHO
Hepatitis B & C
HBV: High risk. HCV: Once for adults 18–79, earlier if risk.
CDC, USPSTF 2020
Tuberculosis
Screen high-risk using TST or IGRA.
WHO, MOH
Depression & Anxiety
All ≥12 yrs: PHQ-9 & GAD-7 annually.
USPSTF, MOH
Smoking & Substance Use
Ask all ≥12 yrs. Counsel and support cessation.
USPSTF, WHO
Premarital Screening
Mandatory before marriage: hemoglobinopathies, HBV, HCV, HIV.
MOH Saudi Program
Osteoporosis
Women ≥65: DEXA. Younger if risk ≥9.3%. Men if risk factors.
USPSTF 2018
