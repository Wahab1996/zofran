Summary

Diabetes mellitus is a group of metabolic diseases characterized by chronic hyperglycemia due to defects in insulin secretion, insulin action, or both. There are two major types: Type 1 (autoimmune Œ≤-cell destruction ‚Üí absolute insulin deficiency) and Type 2 (insulin resistance + relative insulin deficiency). Goals of treatment include glycemic control, prevention of micro- and macrovascular complications, and lifestyle modification.
