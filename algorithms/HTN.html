📈 Hypertension

Definition: Persistent elevation of blood pressure ≥ 140/90 mmHg on 2+ occasions.

🩺 Common Symptoms

  • 😰 Headache (صداع)
  • 😵 Dizziness (دوخة)
  • 👀 Blurred vision (زغللة في النظر)
  • 💔 Chest discomfort or palpitations
  • 🦵 Leg swelling (edema)
  • 🔇 Sometimes asymptomatic — "silent killer"

📊 Risk Factors

  • 🍟 Obesity & sedentary lifestyle
  • 🧂 High salt intake
  • 🧬 Family history
  • 🚬 Smoking
  • 🍷 Excess alcohol
  • 😣 Stress

🧪 Diagnosis

  • 💉 BP measurement ≥140/90 mmHg on at least 2 separate visits
  • 🔬 Basic labs: CBC, electrolytes, creatinine, fasting glucose
  • 🩸 Lipid profile & urinalysis
  • 🫀 ECG (to check for LVH)

🩷 Lifestyle Changes

  • 🥗 DASH diet (low salt, more fruits/veggies)
  • 🏃 Regular exercise (30 min/day)
  • ⚖️ Weight loss if overweight
  • 🚭 Stop smoking
  • 🍷 Limit alcohol

💊 Medications

  • 💧 Thiazide diuretics
  • ❤️ ACE inhibitors / ARBs
  • 🧘 Beta blockers (if indicated)
  • 🫁 Calcium channel blockers

📌 Note: Hypertension is a major risk factor for stroke, heart attack, and kidney disease. Regular follow-up and adherence to treatment are crucial. ✅
