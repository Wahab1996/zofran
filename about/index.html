About This Project

This web app was created to help family medicine doctors access clinical tools, scores, algorithms, references, medications, and screening guidelines quickly and easily in one place.

Our goal is to save time, improve care, and empower family physicians with reliable resources at their fingertips.

If you have suggestions or feedback, feel free to contact the developer.
